    Office Hours

    There are over 600 students in this class, and because of this office hours tend to get fairly crowded, especially the day(s) before a project is due.  To prevent the OH room from getting excessively crowded, we are establishing a few policies for office hours:

    • Students will have 3 tokens for office hours per day.  Once you run out of tokens, you will not be able to join the queue until the following day.
    • Students are NOT allowed to "camp out" in the office hours room.  Once you are helped, you must leave the office hours room.  If you want to get back on the queue, then you may come back
    • We will be making our best attempt to limit our time with each student to 5 minutes.  Obviously, in some cases this will not be possible, but we aren't going to sit with you for extended periods of time and help you debug your code.  Since your time with the TAs is limited, be sure to have a clear understanding of your problem before joining the queue.  You should be able to answer questions like "what have you tried?" and "what line is the error on?"  If you can answer these questions, the 5 minutes we can spend with you will be much more productive.  If your question is more conceptual, you should be more specific than just asking "where do I start?"
    • If we call your name and you aren't there, we will dequeue you and move on.

    TAs will be enforcing these rules at all times, but especially when office hours are crowded.  If a TA asks you to leave and you are not on the queue, you must do so.

    Finally, when you join the queue, please specify a specific "topic".  Something like "I'm having trouble with public test X" or "submission problem" is much more helpful than just saying "project 1".  It will also help you make sure you know exactly what your problem is.
